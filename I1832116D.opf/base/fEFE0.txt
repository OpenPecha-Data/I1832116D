yaḥ punar bhikṣur anvardhamāsaṃ prātimokṣasūtre uddiśyamāne evaṃ vaded idānīm ahaṃ jānāmi āyuṣmanto ’yam api kila dharmaḥ
sūtragataḥ sūtraparyāyāpannoddeśyam āgacchatīti tatrāyuṣmantaṃ ced bhikṣavo jānīyuḥ niṣaṇṇapūrvaṃ āyuṣmatā dvitrikṛtvaṃ
poṣadhe kaḥ punar vādo bhūya iti āyuṣmato ajñānataḥ na muktiḥ yata āpattim āpannaḥ tato yathādharmaṃ kārayitavya uttara
ṃ ca kaukṛtyam āropayitavyaṃ -- āyuṣman te alābho ’labdhaḥ durlabdho na sulabdho yat tvaṃ anvardhamāsaṃ prātimokṣasūtrod
diśyamāne na satkṛtya śṛṇoṣi na gurukaroṣi nārthikaroṣi na manasikaroṣi ekāgracittenāvahitaśrotreṇa na śṛṇoṣi sarvacitta
saṃkalpair api na śṛṇoṣi | āyuṣman tatra kaukṛtyāt pāyantikā |
